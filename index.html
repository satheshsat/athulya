About Me

I am an actress who has appeared in Tamil films. After making my debut with Kadhal Kan Kattudhe, I had gone on to act in films including V. Z. Durai's Yemaali and Samuthirakani's Naadodigal 2.

My native of Coimbatore, studied Information Technology at Karpagam College of Engineering, Coimbatore. I made my acting debut through the college romance film Kadhal Kan Kattudhe, which released on the 2017 Valentines Day weekend. The film won positive reviews, concluding that overall it was "a cool and breezy film that is worth a watch". The film took a low profile opening, but eventually out ran bigger profile films Ennodu Vilayadu and Pagadi Aattam at the Chennai box office.

Movie Timeline

2020

Naadodigal 2

Naadodigal 2 is a 2020 Indian Tamil-language drama film written and directed by Samuthirakani and produced by S Nandagopal under the banner of Madras Enterprises. It stars Sasikumar and Anjali and Subhashini kannan. The film released on 31 January 2020.

Vattam

Vattam is an upcoming Indian Tamil action film written and directed by Kamalakannan.[1] The film stars Sibi Sathyaraj, Nikki Galrani, Andrea Jeremiah and Athulya Ravi in the lead roles.

2019

Suttu Pidikka Utharavu

Suttu Pidikka Utharavu is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language action film starring Vikranth and Athulya Ravi in the lead, alongside directors Suseenthiran and Mysskin. The film is written and directed by Ramprakash Rayappan

Adutha Saattai

Adutha Saattai is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language action drama film which is a sequel to Saattai, written and directed by M. Anbazhagan. The film is the third installment in the Saattai franchise. It stars Samuthirakani and Thambi Ramaiah in the lead roles with Athulya Ravi

Capmaari

Capmaari is a 2019 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy film written and directed by S. A. Chandrasekhar. The film stars Jai, Athulya Ravi, and Vaibhavi Shandilya in lead roles.

2018

Yemaali

Yemaali is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language romantic comedy drama film written and directed by V. Z. Durai.

Nagesh Thiraiyarangam

Nagesh Thiraiyarangam is a 2018 Indian Tamil-language horror drama film produced by Rajendra M. Rajan and Punitha Rajan for Transindia Media & Entertainment Private Limited and directed by Mohamad Issack.

2017

Kadhal Kan Kattudhe

Kadhal Kan Kattudhe (English: Love is Dazzling) is a 2017 Indian Tamil romantic drama film written, co-produced and directed by Shivaraj.

Katha Nayagan

Katha Nayagan (English: Lead Hero) is a 2017 Indian Tamil romantic comedy film written and directed by Tha. Muruganantham. Produced by Vishnu. I play the support role.
